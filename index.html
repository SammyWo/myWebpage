Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT? How did your interest in IT start? Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? Outline IT Experience (if any).

IT has played a significant role all my life as I grew up in the advent of every household owning a computer. Now we carry computing devices wherever we go. My interest in IT first started when I was very young, perhaps 3 or 4 years old when my father purchased an Amiga computer. At the time the technology seemed so monumental and the playing games on the Amiga (despite the now incredibly rudimentary graphics) sparked a great deal of imagination and wonder. Later in life I became involved in music production and recording I started to use a Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) named Ardour.

Ardour is an open source application that permits the users to make modifications and adjustments to help improve the software. I learned that gitHub is the main tool for collaborators used to access and modify source code (Ardour/ardour, 2020). Through gitHub I was able to learn who these collaborators were and what programming languages they used such as python, C++, C and Javascript. While I had little understanding about many of the tools and techniques used in developing software, seeing all the work and effort that goes into developing such software made me appreciate the skills necessary for such a project.

Witnessing the community actively work together to make something special truly inspired me and rekindled some of the fascination with IT that I had when I was younger. Despite having limited previous IT experience and no experience developing software, I hope to one day be able to develop software and perhaps contribute to a project.
